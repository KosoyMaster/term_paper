About me

Specialize :
I specialize in lifestyle and travel photography. I not only shoot in and around the streets of the city, but also in apartments, shops, parks – anywhere and everywhere my clients FEEL happy.
Why :
Finding places that make people feel happy, as well as creating memories while we shoot. And that is what makes great photos!
For whom :
My work in photography is a natural extension of my desire to tell stories. I’ve always been a nostalgic and romantic person, and I love that my work allows me to express those traits in such a meaningful way.
